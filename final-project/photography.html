My Film Photos

Film has a distinct and unique look that many people find visually appealing. It offers a different color rendition, tonal range, and grain structure compared to digital images. I believe that film adds charm and a nostolgic feel that digital cameras cannot capture.

Not only that, but when you take pictures on a film camera, I believe that it is a more intential experience. You only have so many pictures you are allowed to take on a roll, forcing you to slow down and appreciate the moment. When you get your photographs back, it is so fun to see what your camera took and it is such a rewarding experience.

This past Christmas, I received a film camera and it has been my enw pride and joy. I have been taking the photos, and getting them developing at my local camera store. I bought a scrapbook to hold them and I love this new hobby! To share it with y'all, down below is a collection of some of my favorite photos I have taken. :)
