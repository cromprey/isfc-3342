about me!

I'm Caitlin!

Hello! I am a student-athlete at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. I am pursuing my degree in Web Design and Development, with a double minor in information technology and graphic arts.

When I am not at class or at practice, you can find me exploring new places, trying out new foods, spending times with my family and friends. I’m a little shy at first, but I love making connections and meeting new people.

Some of my hobbies include reading, watching movies, drawing, and taking pictures on my vintage film camera. I love the feeling of taking a picture and not knowing what you’re gonna get until you develop it and can hold it in your hand. I have been making a scrapbook for my film photos because I think it will be fun to look back on it.

Overall, I think that I am a pretty optimistic person and I believe in living life to the fullest. I love embracing new experience and seeing the best in opportunities. I’m excited to keep making this little blog, and see where this little journey takes me. Thanks for stopping by!
